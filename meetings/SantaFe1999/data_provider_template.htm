the Santa Fe Convention: Template for a data provider
 
The Santa Fe Convention is discontinued. Please use the Open Archives Initiative Protocol for Metadata Harvesting instead.

Instructions for use

  • Extract template part of this HTML page - the part between the horizontal rules - and save it as a separate HTML page.
  • Remove the italicized text from the righthand column of the table and fill out the appropriate information instead.
  • Install the resulting page on your web server.
  • Send an e-mail to openarchives@openarchives.org to inform the Open Archives initiative of its URL.
  • The Open Archives initiative will list your service and the URL of the your completed data provider template at http://www.openarchives.org/sfc/sfc_archives.htm.
