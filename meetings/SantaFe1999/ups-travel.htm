Flight arrangements to Albuquerque

Book your own flight to attend the meeting. Participants that have required a travel stipend should keep a proof of payement. It is recommended to fly into Albuquerque - New Mexico - Airport. The Santa Fe Airport is very small, and it will still be a pricey cab ride to the hotel from that Airport.

From Albuquerque to Santa Fe

It is approximately a 70 minutes drive from the Albuquerque Airport to the Santa Fe hotels. You may elect:

  • To rent a car at the airport. Still, no activities are planned during the UPS meeting that would require a car while in Santa Fe.
  • To take a shuttle to the hotel:

SANDIA SHUTTLE EXPRESS

  • Call 505-243-3244 for reservations. Confirm with credit card.
  • Vans depart from Albuquerque Airport in front of Southwest Airlines, ground level. When boarding the van, please tell your driver the name of the hotel. If you do not have a credit card reservation; the driver can accept cash or traveler’s checks. The charge is $20.and correct change is appreciated. DRIVERS CANNOT ACCEPT CREDIT CARDS.
  • Schedule from Albuquerque to Santa Fe: AM 8:35 9:30 10:50 PM 12:15 1:50 3:10 3:50 5:30 6:50 8:45

GRAY LINE of ALBUQUERQUE

  • Call 1-800-256-8991 for reservations and to prepay with credit card.
  • Vans depart from from Albuquerque Airport at the baggage claim level of the airport, from the commercial lanes. Charge is $25.
  • Schedule from Albuquerque to Santa Fe: AM 9:00 11:00 PM 1:00 4:00 6:00 9:00

Hotel information

  • The UPS participants will stay at the Fort Marcy Hotel Suites in Santa Fe. Visit them at http://www.santafehotels.com . Lodging has been reserved and confirmed for all participants from the afternoon of October 20, thru the afternoon of October 22. Participants that want to extend their stay on their own account should immediately call to try to find a room. Call Fort Marcy Hotel Suites at 1-800-745-9910. October is one of the most popular months to visit and the city will be full.
  • Meals will be provided from the morning of October 21 thru the afternoon of October 22.
  • The hotel is on ten landscaped acres, four blocks from the historic Plaza, overlooking the city. The hotel offers van service to the Santa Fe Plaza and it is also a delightful downhill walk. Each suite has two bedrooms, each with full private bath. Occupants will share a fully equiped kitchen and living room. The suites reflect the Santa Fe "style" and most suites have fireplaces and private balconies or patios. The hotel has a small indoor pool, but is just two blocks away from a very large community fitness facility, with lap swimming, tennis courts, etc.
  • Santa Fe is at over 7,000 ft altitude in the high desert of New Mexico, in the foothills of the Sangre de Cristo mountains. In October, nights will be very cool, but days should be warm and sunny. A jacket and sweater are needed. We will be working together in a casual atmosphere. Ties are not required :-)
