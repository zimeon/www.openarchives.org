Agenda for the Cornell meeting of the Technical Committee of the Open Archives initiative, September 7-8 2000

__________________

The meeting aims at an in depth revision of the Santa Fe convention of the Open Archives initiative in light of:

  • Experiences obtained with the implementation of the SFc;
  • The emerging interest for the application of SFc-concepts as a general interoperability framework for scholarly datasets.

The meeting is supported by the Digital Library Federation.

Expenses

The Digital Library Federation supports this meeting [many thanks to Dan Greenstein!]. The formula being used is similar to the one we used for the Santa Fe meeting: The DLF will cover reasonable room and board for all as well as travel expenses for those who require it, i.e. for those who can not get travel support via their own institutions or other funding.

Make sure to keep all your expense receipts. In order to be refundable by DLF, you will have to fill out a form [which we will provide] and to deliver your receipts.

Travel

Ithaca has an airport served by one airline, US Airways. Flights to Pittsburg are real planes (DC9 jets), flights to everywhere else are props with flights to and from Philadelphia being the wonderful 19 seater Beechcraft (noise, bump, vibrate, and some terror for those inclined towards claustraphobia). A nearby bigger airport is Syracuse with more airlines - 1.5 hours away. If you decide to stay over Saturday night we can direct you to things to do.
