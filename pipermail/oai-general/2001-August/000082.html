Jim,           You are right, all statements to date from the Chemistry 
Preprint Server on OAi seem to be vague, professing support but not 
implementation yet. The key point perhaps, especially as this is a preprint 
rather than an eprint archive, is whether the journals, say those that 
accept papers from the CPS, are willing to make metadata available through 
OAi. There is no reason why they shouldn't, since for the last year OAi has 
explicitly had a digital library scope rather than an eprint scope. Except 
the publishers will have noted there appears to be value in the metadata, 
currently being realised by CrossRef. The only publisher, as far as I am 
aware, that is planning to make journals data available through OAi is 
BioMed Central. Are there others?
