                CALL FOR PARTICIPATION ECDL 2001

            5th European Conference on Research and 
      	   Advanced Technology for Digital Libraries

4-8 September 2001
Darmstadt, Germany

http://www.ecdl2001.org

This year's program includes seven workshops, four
tutorials, three invited talks, three special panels,
thirty-eight scientific papers in nine sessions, plus
posters and demos.  Excursions are planned for
Thursday, 6 September.

Conference registration and hotel reservations can be
performed electronically via the conference's website
(click on Registration, then Conference Registration).
Please keep in mind the contingent of reserved hotel
rooms is limited.
