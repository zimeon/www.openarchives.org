**OAI Data Providers: General**

I am pleased to announce the publication and availability of my 
latest eProfile column from _Library Hi Tech News_:

Gerry McKiernan. "Open Archives Initiative Data Providers. Part I.
General," 
_Library Hi Tech News_ 21 no. 3 (April 2004): 11-19.

A copy of the article has been self-archived at

http://www.public.iastate.edu/~gerrymck/OAI-DP-I.pdf

[ http://eprints.rclis.org/ ]
