[II] "Gaining Independence with E-Print Archives and OAI: Report on
the 2nd Workshop on the Open Archives Initiative, 
October 17-19, 2002, CERN, Geneva, Switzerland," _Journal of Internet
Cataloging_ 6, no. 3 (2003) [2004]: 35-52.
