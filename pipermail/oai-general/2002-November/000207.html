Dear Colleague,

_OCLC Systems & Services_ would like to invite potential authors to
contribute articles related to XML for ejournals for the v. 19, no. 4 issue.
Dr.Judith Wusteman will be the editor for this special issue. The focus of
this issue includes all aspects of the use of XML for ejournal production,
dissemination, display and archiving such as

1.	XML and e-journal archives
2.	XML for e-journal metadata and full article text
3.	XML and the journal production lifecycle
4. 	The application of SVG, MathML, CML etc to ejournals

The submission deadline for this special issue is May 1, 2003.  If you are
interested in submitting your work, please send your manuscript in MS Word
or RTF format to Dr.Judith Wusteman at <judith.wusteman@ucd.ie> or
Sheau-Hwang Chang at <schang@bridgew.edu>.  

_OCLC Systems & Services_ is a refereed and quaterly publication with an
international readership. Please visist its home page at
<<http://www.emeraldinsight.com/oclc.htm> for more information. 

I look forward to hearing from you.
