For info...

The Distributed National Electronic Resource (DNER) is a significant UK
initiative to build a national digital library to support research and
learning within the UK higher and further education community.

  http://www.jisc.ac.uk/dner/

The OAI Metadata Harvesting Protocol is one of several components in the
technical architecture that underpins the development of the DNER

  http://www.ukoln.ac.uk/distributed-systems/dner/arch/
