Hi Jody,

There are four:

Library of Congress Digitized Historical Collections
http://memory.loc.gov/cgi-bin/oai2_0

Indiana University Digital Library Program
http://oai.dlib.indiana.edu/phpoai/oai2.php
(certain sets)

OCLC Research Publications
http://errol.oclc.org/orpubs.oclc.org.srw2oai

University of Chicago Library Metadata Repository
http://oai.lib.uchicago.edu/
(certain sets)

You can see them in action at our test MODS portal (work in progress...):
http://www.hti.umich.edu/m/mods/

- Kat
