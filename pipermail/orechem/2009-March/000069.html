Hi all

I'll be visiting for the meeting (and ER conference) as a member of Jane
Hunter's eResearch Group at The University Queensland.

I've been working with the Structural Genomics group at UQ, and have
developed a laboratory information management system for capturing
experiment data (eg cloning, expression, purification, crystallisation).
I have also started developing a diffraction image archive as part of
the <http://tardis.edu.au/> project, with the aim of exposing datasets
using ORE. The ultimate aim is to also use ORE for linking between
objects in these systems, with further linking to PDB, PubMed, etc.

You can find notes about our research at
<http://services.nsdl.org/trac/oreChem/wiki/UniOfQld>.
