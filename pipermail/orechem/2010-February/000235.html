Cloud Futures 2010
Advancing Research with Cloud Computing
April 8-9, 2010
Redmond, WA

The workshop invites talks from industry and academia that illustrate the role of cloud computing across a variety of research and curriculum development areas---including  computer science, earth sciences, healthcare, humanities, life sciences, and social sciences.

Details at: http://research.microsoft.com/en-us/events/cloudfutures2010/default.aspx
