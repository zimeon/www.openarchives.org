Dear all,

In preparation of next week's in-person meeting of the ORE Technical 
Committee, we have prepared a thoroughly revised version of the document 
"Compound Information Objects: The OAI-ORE Perspective", which we shared 
with you a while ago.

As a matter of fact, this is really a completely reworked version, 
introducing a new perspective to look at the compound object problem 
(named graphs), and detailing the issues that require resolution in 
order for OAI-ORE to be able to build an interoperability layer based on 
that perspective.

This document, along with reports of experiments conducted by sub-groups 
of the ORE Technical Committee form the basic input for next week's meeting.

As always, your feedback is very much appreciated.

Greetings

Herbert and Carl
