hi

You are invited to attend a full-day workshop of the Open Archives
Initiative, to be held as part of the ECDL2000 conference in Lisbon,
Portugal on 21 September 2000.

This workshop will continue the infrastructural, technical and policy
discussions initiated at San Antonio, with additional feedback sought
from new participants.
(see article in June 2000 D-Lib Magazine for summary of San Antonio
workshop - http://www.dlib.org/dlib/june00/06inbrief.html#FOX)

Further details can be found on the workshop website at:
  http://purl.org/net/oaisept00/

Registration for ECDL and the workshop can be done through:
  http://www.bn.pt/org/agenda/ecdl2000/
Note that early registration ends on July 17th!

More details will be posted to the website and mailing list when they
are finalized.

(my apologies if you get multiple copies of this - i am sending it
generally to the UPS mailing list and specifically to everyone who
attended the San Antonio workshop)
