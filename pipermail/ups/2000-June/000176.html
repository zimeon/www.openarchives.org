Open Archives Initiative Technical Working Group
------------------------------------------------

Membership: 
Open to all those who can actively contribute to the work of the
Technical Working Group (namely: evaluate, define, prototype and test
the current/next version of the OAi protocol/specifications).

NOTE: Joining this group requires a commitment from members to:-
- actively evaluate the current and proposed work
- prototype and implement new protocols for testing purposes
- engage in specification-writing activities

Initially, most discussion will take place on the mailing list set up
for this purpose. To join the mailing list, send email to
listserv@listserv.vt.edu with the following body:
  subscribe OAI-TECH-L
You will receive further instructions from the server.
