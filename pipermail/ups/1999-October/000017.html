I agree with Steven that we should have a journal metadata 
field. This field could indicate the journal title the 
article is "submitted to" since most authors are likely to 
know where they plan to submit their article. Once 
published, they may return to update the specific volume, 
issue and page information, or they may return to change 
the title if a different jounal publishes the article. I do 
not think we should mix up journal titles and subjects. I 
think this could lead to very "dirty data".
