Here's another vote for 

  Interconnected Eprint Archives    (with no objection to alternative
                                     capitalizations)

and against putting journal name into Subject.  I'm marginally in favor of
having it in the Comment element, in order to reserve the ability to use a
separate element down the road to support a controlled form of journal
title.  Variability in abbreviated journal titles will be a fact of life
for data entered by users without tools providing lists to pick from.
