Hi!

PART 1 - NSF ITR Pre-proposal
As promised, a pre-proposal was sent to NSF to support
OAI. Thanks go to the many who provided input.

See the attached PDF file which is the 1 page summary sent.
If anyone wants more - e.g., the 5 page description, or even
more detail - please let me know. Since the NSF ITR program
is competitive and many of you may be involved in proposals,
I'm trying to be sensitive and am not just broadcasting the whole
pre-proposal to people who don't want to see it.

On the other hand, I'd really like a substantive letter of
support by EVERYONE involved in any way with OAI.  Assuming we
go forward past the pre-proposal stage, the full proposal is due
April 17. I'd like support letters before April 1 if possible, 
giving details, and dollar value of in-kind contributions where
that can be documented (else assessment of who will spend what
level of effort).


PART 2 - OAI-related workshop June 3, San Antonio
Today I received one note indicating that our proposal for an
OAI related workshop has been approved and that we will be
scheduled for June 3, a Saturday, that is in between
  - ACM Hypertext '2000          http://www.ht00.org/
  - ACM Digital Libraries '2000  http://www.dl00.org/
and so should draw attendees not only from our current group
but potentially from those interested in either of those events.
More on this later when I get details from the conference organizers.
But please put this date on your calendars!

Regards, Ed
