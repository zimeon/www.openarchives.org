Attached please find a PDF file version of the 1 page summary (to be followed,
when I finish writing it, by 5 page project description) that I intend to
submit on

do welcome and encourage collaboration with same.  I hope we can find other
funds to extend this effort to help those groups, and am very grateful for the
expressions of cost-sharing support already received from same.

Please note that University of Virginia, which has very active e-text work
and has
been collaborating with Cornell for a number of years, as well as CalTech,
will be
major partners.  Carl only wants a small amount of support.  U. Kentucky is
from
an EPSCOR state, which NSF particularly likes, and will have a small effort
focussed on surveys and other evaluation.

This is due on 1/5, so I hope to get comments ASAP. Many thanks, Ed
