Hussein Suleman (<hussein@vt.edu>) just pointed out that I had
implemented the revised 'accession' and 'discovery' tags in the
Disseminate reply incorrectly. They now have the correct form
of `accession' tag with 'date' attribute (example below).

Cheers,
Simeon.
