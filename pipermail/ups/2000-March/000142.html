Thomas Krichel has volunteered to speak on OAi at the CERN meeting (see
mail by Corrado Pettenati).  I would like to support his proposal. 
Still, I think that Thomas will have to clearly distinguish between the
agreed-upon OAi/SF-convention principles and principles used in the
RePEc-world.  The core of the presentation should be promoting and
explaining OAi and SF-convention.  I feel that it is very important to
do this at the CERN meeting.
