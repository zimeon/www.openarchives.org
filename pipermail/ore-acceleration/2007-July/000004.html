Hi all:

 

My guess is that people "registered" for the blog but didn't "subscribe".  So
please do the following as soon as possible:

 

1.        Go to http://ore.blogging.com/

2.       Login under your user name and password

3.       Click on "My Preferences" in the upper right menu

4.       In the "Notification" section select "For all articles" for both
"Receive article email notices" and "Receive comment email notices"

 

Also you can syndicate the feed from the link on the blog home page.

 

Let me know if you have any problems.

 

Carl
