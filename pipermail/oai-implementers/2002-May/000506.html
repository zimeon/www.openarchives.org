Hello, all -- we are now open for registering/validating OAI
repositories
at the Version 2.0 level.  In fact, one site has already been registered
as
a version 2 repository.

One change from version 1 is that the notion of compliant and robust
has been dropped.  Compliant sites are expected to recognize and handle
the six verbs as well as the following 13 erroneous requests:

?junk
?verb=junk
?verb=GetRecord&metadataPrefix=oai_dc
?verb=GetRecord&identifier=(some valid id from your repo)
?verb=GetRecord&identifier=invalid"id&metadataPrefix=oai_dc
?verb=ListIdentifiers&until=junk
?verb=ListIdentifiers&from=junk
?verb=ListIdentifiers&resumptionToken=junk&until=2000-02-05
?verb=ListRecords
?verb=ListRecords&resumptionToken=junk
?verb=ListRecords&metadataPrefix=oai_dc&ResumptionToken=junk&until=1990-01-10

?verb=ListRecords&metadataPrefix=oai_dc&until=junk
?verb=ListRecords&metadataPrefix=oai_dc&from=junk
