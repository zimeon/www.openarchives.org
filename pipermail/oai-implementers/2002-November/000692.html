The UK JISC-funded RoMEO Project (Rights metadata for open-archiving) is
performing a survey of OAI Data and Service Providers regarding the IPR
issues they face.  If you are a Data or Service Provider - no matter how
new - we would be very grateful if you could take the time to complete our
online questionnaires at:

Data Providers Questionnaire:
http://www-staff.lboro.ac.uk/~lbeag/Data-provider-questionnaire.htm
Service Providers Questionnaire:
http://www-staff.lboro.ac.uk/~lbeag/Service-provider-questionnaire.htm

For further information about the RoMEO project please see our web pages at
http://www.lboro.ac.uk/departments/ls/disresearch/romeo/index.html
