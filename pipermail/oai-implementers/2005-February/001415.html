  Mark your calendars!

  The next CERN workshop on Innovations in Scholarly Communication (OAI4)
  will be held at CERN, Geneva, Switzerland from 20 to 22 October 2005.
  This will be the latest installment in this highly succesful series
  that began in 2001. It brings together activists who are at the
  forefront of scholarly communication change. The organizing
  committee will be issuing a call for contributions soon. 

  Please distribute this announcement as you see fit.
