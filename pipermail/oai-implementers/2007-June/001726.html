hi,

I am happy to inform you that the document "Compound Information 
Objects: An OAI-ORE Perspective" is now available at 
<http://www.openarchives.org/ore/documents/CompoundObjects-200705.html>.

This document introduces a new perspective to look at compound 
information objects within the web environment, and details the issues 
that require resolution in order for OAI-ORE to be able to build an 
interoperability layer based on that perspective. This document formed 
the basis for last week's discussions of the ORE Technical Committee.

Feedback is welcome at ore at openarchives.org.
