I would like to draw your attention to the following paper which discusses an 
OAI-PMH interface and an OpenURL interface to Open Archival Information Systems:

Bekaert, Jeroen, and Herbert Van de Sompel. Access Interfaces for Open Archival 
Information Systems based on the OAI-PMH and the OpenURL Framework for 
Context-Sensitive Services. 2005. Preprint at 
http://arxiv.org/abs/cs.DL/0509090. Draft of an accepted submission for PV 2005 
"Ensuring Long-term Preservation and Adding Value to Scientific and Technical 
data". see http://www.ukoln.ac.uk/events/pv-2005/ for conference information.
