Stuart,

Indeed there is not yet a xml schema for qualified DC.  Encouraged by the success of (finally) producing a schema for unqualified Dublin Core (the one referenced in the OAI-PMH v2 specification at http://dublincore.org/schemas/xmls/simpledc20020312.xsd) a number of us in the DC and OAI community are in the midst of producing such a schema.  The timetable is for a solid proposal middle of next month with closure on the process by October.
