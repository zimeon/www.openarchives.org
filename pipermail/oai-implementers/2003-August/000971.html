Todd White wrote:

> while we're on the subject of the required oai_dc format, i'm hoping
> someone(s) can verify for me that the following XML for the
> standard/required oai_dc format is NOT valid.  (the Open Archives
> Initiative - Repository Explorer already did, but any further
> validation/comments will be most appreciated.)
> 
> specifically, the attribute/values -- xsi:type="dct:DCMIType" and
> xsi:type="dct:URI" -- are not allowed, correct?

It is definitely not valid.  It looks they are trying to use Qualifed DC 
which is not allowed by the simple oai_dc XML Schema. There are XML Schema 
for Qualified Dublin Core that use the xsi:type attribute to specific 
Qualifed DC encoding schemes.  See 
http://www.dublincore.org/schemas/xmls/qdc/2003/04/02/notes/ for details. 
If the provider wanted to provide Qualifed DC with the xsi:type attributes 
they could use one of the container schema provided from the above URL.
