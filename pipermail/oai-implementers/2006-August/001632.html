Is this a CONTENTdm system?  'oai.exe' often indicates CONTENTdm, but 
not always.

Earlier versions of CONTENTdm did not have support for resumptionToken, 
but recent versions have.  If you have an old version of CONTENTdm you 
may need to upgrade.
