A better way to determine "chunk size":  
 
1.  figure out your average record size (in bytes)
2.  pick a number so response size is between .5 and 2 MB 
 
see:  http://oai-best.comm.nsdl.org/cgi-bin/wiki.pl?ResumptionTokens
