I hear your point and its a matter of philosophy.  Our goal in the
registry and in the conformance testing is to provide a relatively high
integrity list of data providers (where integrity is measured by our
stated test criteria).  The list of old data providers while
historically interesting doesn't provide any information that anyone can
use - e.g., Santa Fe conformance has really very little to do with OAI
1.0 conformance.  Being a minimalist at heart I'd rather stick to
posting information that is useful for applications.

>

service providers of choice. Hopefully there will be an increasing
number of service providers tailored for individual community needs.
