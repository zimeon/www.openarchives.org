Hi all,
please excuse my late answer on Martin's request for venue information.

Now here it comes...


The venue of our meeting will be the "Residenzschloss" (Building S3/13,
Marktplatz 15), in the city center not far away from the ECDL conference

venue

Have a look at http://www.soziologie.de/iuk2000/pics/map.jpg
(the venue marked as "Schloss", the trainstation is "Hauptbahnhof")

and at http://www.tu-darmstadt.de/lageplaene/darmstadt/stadtmitte/s3.tud

(where you can find Building S3/13)

>From the trainstation take Bus D direction "Ostbahnhof" or Bus F
direction "Oberwaldhaus" and get of at stop "Schloss", which is about a
7 minutes tour.

>From the ECDL conference venue it is even more convenient to take a bus

from bus stop "Berliner Alee". There you take the tram 9 or 10 in
direction "Böllenfalltor". Or take bus K direction Jaupstrasse, bus H
direction Parkstrasse or the two above mentioned busses.

In the building (Residenzschloss)  you have to find
"Institut fuer Soziologie/Philosophie" in the third floor.
The Roomnumber is 319. There will be
signs showing you the way.


Best wishes,
