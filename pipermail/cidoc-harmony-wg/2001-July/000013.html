Dear collegues,

I want to make the application for the next DELOS-Harmony meeting
on Ontology Harmonization. Goal will be the actual comparison of the
two models, validation of the proposed methodology, initializing the
work on the White Paper, decision on further actions. 
I shall work on the detailed agenda later. For the moment, I need
confirmation about who participates (Sept. 8, 9) :

PLEASE send me, as soon as possible, your participation confirmation.
Please keep in mind, that hotel rooms in Darmstadt are running out soon.

BTW, during the ICOM conference, the CIDOC CRM Special Interest Group,
which represents officially the CRM work, expressed its strong interest in
this collaboration with Harmony. Minutes will be published soon
on http://cidoc.ics.forth.gr. CRM is now registered as: ISO/AWI21127,
and the CRM version 3.1 has been formally accepted as new discussion base.

Please note also, that the Web-Site in Geneva: 
http://www.ville-ge.ch/musinfo/cidoc/oomodel/index.htm
is no more maintained. By decision of CIDOC in Ottawa, 2000, 
the contents have been completely transferred to http://cidoc.ics.forth.gr.
(http://cidoc.ics.forth.gr/reports.html, Minutes from Ottawa, point 8) 
Please update your links.

Martin
