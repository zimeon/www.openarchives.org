Dear All,

We had the first meeting of the CIDOC CRM SIG in July 5th in
Barcelona. The CRM SIG is the group in CIDOC which has the mandate
to speak for the CRM. The report of the first meeting is up on 
the Website (http://cidoc.ics.forth.gr/docs/cidoc_crm_first_meeting.doc).
The Group confirmed the strong interest in working towards a common
model with ABC Harmony.
The scope of the CRM has been identified, and is published:
http://cidoc.ics.forth.gr/crm_scope_definition.html

I attach to this message comments and thoughts about 
Carl's and Jane's paper, as a preparation for the discussion in
Darmstadt. 

Those that haven't done yet, please confirm if they will come to
Darmstadt.
