The Virtual Planetary Exhibition (VPE) has been elaborated to celebrate =
the bicentennial of the birth of Sir Henry Cole creator of the first =
Universal Exhibition. The principle of the VPE is to comply with Sir =
Cole's phrase: "Learn how to see and see through comparison". To do =
that, in several countries, will be organised real events showing the =
local know-how techniques in the domains of arts, culture, science, =
industry or craftsmanship. Each of these events will be broadcast, in =
real time, through an central operational headquarter located in a =
capital city yet to be determined.=20

One of the vocations of the Club TIP is to organise international =
events. The latest example is the Digit World Contest, accessible =

first edition, the DWC has gathered 744 participating web sites from 79 =
countries, all devoted to conservation and promotion of artistic and =
cultural heritage.=20

virtual world in a single manifestation. The VPE have the ambition to =
become an international observatory for the survey of the evolution of =
the technologies of interactive information and communication. The VPE =
shall display the human aspect of these technologies. The VPE shall be =
eventually an interactive bond between peoples.=20
