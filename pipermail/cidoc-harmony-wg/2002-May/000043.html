Agenda:

   "Dinosaur bone example"  by Martin

            - important references,
            - authors
      c) Description of CRM/ABC:

            - authors
      d) The Merging:
            - topics to be covered,
            - structure of subsections
            - agreement on what are the key lessons learned
            - agreement on conclusions about the applied method
                     may be automatic versus intellectual merging?
            - important references,
            - authors
      e) Research Issues, Future Work:
            - topics to be covered,
            - relations to other modelling activities
            - important references
            - authors
      f) Glossary:
            - structure and terms to be covered
            -authors
     g) Bibliography
            - how to gather input from all partners, in which form
                     (e.g. commented wrt sections/phrases in the Report,
                        further reading etc.)
            - authors

           a) dissemination/ papers to be produced
