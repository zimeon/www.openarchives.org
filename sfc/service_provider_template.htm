the Santa Fe Convention: Template for a service provider

 

The Santa Fe Convention is discontinued. Please use the Open Archives Initiative Protocol for Metadata Harvesting instead.

Instructions for use

  • Extract template part of this HTML page - the part between the horizontal rules - and save it as a separate HTML page.
  • Remove the ilalicized text from the righthand column of the table and fill out the appropriate information instead.
  • Install the resulting page on your archive web server.
  • Send an e-mail to openarchives@openarchives.org to inform the Open Archives initiative on its URL.
  • The Open Archives initiative will list your archive and the URL of your completed service provider template at http://www.openarchives.org/sfc/sfc_services.htm.
